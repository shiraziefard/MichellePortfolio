About

If you have reached this website, congratulations! You have spelled Shiraziefard right, or most likely copied and pasted it from somewhere else. Michelle Shiraziefard is a working artist. Originally aspiring to be a marine biologist, she brought over her affinity for documenting the natural world into her craft. Skilled in organization, conflict resolution and emotional control, her body of work is focused on motifs of socioeconomic status, as well as the intersectionality amongst ethnicty, gender and other factors.
